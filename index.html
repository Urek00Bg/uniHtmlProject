Our Services

Income Management

Easily set your primary monthly budget and log additional income, such as bonuses or side hustles, to keep your totals accurate.

Fixed Expense Planning

Plan for the essentials by logging fixed costs like rent, loans, and utility bills before you spend a single cent of your discretionary income.

Safety & Savings

Use dynamic sliders to set a percentage for "Risk Funds" and savings goals, ensuring you're protected against unplanned events.

Interactive Tracker

Monitor your available balance in real-time and manage your history through a detailed table with full delete and edit capabilities.

About Us

At SmartWallet we prioritize your financial well-being. Our mission is to empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to take control of your budget and make informed financial decisions. What started off as a university project, has evolved into a comprehensive platform designed to meet the needs of users from all walks of life.
